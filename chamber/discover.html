The Greater Nebo Area

We are proud to serve the greater nebo area with our chamber of commerce.

Towns and regions contained in the greater nebo area are:

  • Nephi, UT (pop. 6,600)
  • Mona, UT (pop. 1,815)
  • Levan, UT (pop. 872)
  • Dog Valley, UT (pop. 132)
  • Rocky Ridge, UT (pop. 938)

More Information:

The Nebo Chamber of commerce was established in 2002 by the former mayor, John Smith. Since then we have been hapily connecting buisness owners and the nebo community together! Originally just serving the Nephi and Mona areas, we quickly realized the need for small business owners in other juab county locations to connect with eachother, and have a common place for local residents to look for businesses in their area that have strong standards and a great sense of comminity. Since 2005 we have served Levan, and in 2010 the first business in Dog Valley and Rocky Ridge joined the Commerce.
